Extreme Sports

Extreme sports, also known as action sports or adventure sports, encompass a range of activities that involve a high degree of risk, skill,
and excitement. These sports often push the limits of physical and mental endurance, combining athleticism with adrenaline.

Water Based

Performed in, on, or around
bodies of water. Examples include surfing, where riders ride waves on a board, and wakeboarding, where athletes are towed behind a boat on a board.

Land Based
